Une fois terminé, il sera entièrement traduit en français et mis à disposition dans sa version finale. The Aurora initiative spans multiple teams, environments, and responsibilities. To maintain clarity and consistency, we define a set of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for common workflows, technical conventions, and collaboration practices. SOPs ensure that everyone — from platform engineers to partner departments — can work with a shared understanding of how we operate.

  • Issue & PR Naming Conventions: Standardized naming and labeling for tracking work.
  • Enterprise Landing Zone: Discuss the steps to properly onboard onto the Enterprise Landing Zone for a given C.S.P.
  • Bootstrap Cluster and Transfer Control: Leveraging the bootstrap cluster and transfer of control.
