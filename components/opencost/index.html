OpenCost Also known as: Kubernetes Cost Monitoring OpenCost is used within Aurora to provide transparent, real-time visibility into the cost of running Kubernetes workloads. It allocates infrastructure and cloud resource costs to namespaces, workloads, and labels, enabling platform operators and client teams to understand consumption patterns, support chargeback or showback models, and make informed decisions about resource usage. OpenCost promotes financial accountability while remaining vendor-neutral and aligned with open-source cost management practices. Overview Category: Platform Management
