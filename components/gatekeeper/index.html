Gatekeeper Also known as: OPA Admission Controller Gatekeeper is used within Aurora as the Kubernetes admission controller for enforcing policy and governance controls at resource creation and update time. Built on Open Policy Agent (OPA), Gatekeeper enables policy-as-code enforcement for security, compliance, and platform guardrails, ensuring that Kubernetes resources adhere to approved standards before they are admitted into the cluster. This approach reduces operational risk and supports consistent enforcement of enterprise and regulatory requirements. Overview Category: Security

Architecture Role Acts as the admission-time policy enforcement layer for Kubernetes, intercepting API requests and evaluating them against OPA-based constraint templates and policies before resources are persisted.
